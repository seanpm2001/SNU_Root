/==========\
TERMS OF USE
============\
SNU P2P SERVER SYSTEM
====================/

1. Privacy must remain absolute
When you create a portal, you must provide ethical privacy practices. Companies that violate users privacy with their own portals will be fined, and will have their portal revoked.
Privacy violations include but are not limited to:

* Recording audio without consent
* Recording video without consent
* Collecting user data in any form without consent
* Reading search history

This system is put in place to prevent abuse of power and to protect users who use the SNU P2P server system.
